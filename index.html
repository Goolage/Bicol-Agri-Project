打造 Bicol 水果黃金走廊 的 8 公頃示範園區 Creating an 8-hectare Fruit Gold Corridor in Bicol

結合台灣在芒果、鳳梨、釋迦等熱帶水果上的 優質品種與栽培技術 ，在菲律賓 Bicol 地區打造具示範效益的 8 公頃農業開發計畫，兼具 穩定收益 與 區域農業升級。 Leveraging Taiwan’s expertise in mango, pineapple, and sugar apple premium varieties and cultivation know-how, this 8-hectare project in Bicol is designed as a demonstration farm that balances stable returns and regional agricultural upgrading.

Bicol 8 公頃農業開發基地 Bicol 8-ha Agricultural Development Site

基地位於菲律賓 Bicol 區域，擁有適合熱帶水果的 充足日照、適度雨量與排水地形，結合台灣水果產業多年累積的品種選育、嫁接、修剪及病蟲害管理經驗，打造兼具示範與收益的現代化果園。 The site is located in the Bicol region of the Philippines, with ample sunshine, suitable rainfall, and well-drained terrain ideal for tropical fruits. Together with Taiwan’s long-term experience in variety selection, grafting, pruning and pest management, it will become a modern orchard that delivers both demonstration value and attractive returns.

示範園區角色 Role of the demo farm

本計畫的 8 公頃土地不僅是生產基地，更是展示 「台灣技術 × 菲律賓土地」的最佳案例，讓投資人能以具體數字與現場成果評估長期布局。 The 8-ha site is not only a production base but also a flagship case of “Taiwanese technology × Philippine land”, giving investors concrete data and on-site results to evaluate long-term positioning.

台灣優質水果品種與技術優勢 Premium Taiwanese Fruit Varieties & Technical Edge

根據當地土壤與氣候條件，優先導入適應性佳且具市場辨識度的台灣芒果、鳳梨與釋迦品種，並搭配精準施肥、病蟲害整合管理與採後處理技術，提升整體產值。 Based on local soil and climate, we introduce well-adapted Taiwanese mango, pineapple and sugar apple varieties with strong market recognition, supported by precise fertilization, integrated pest management and post-harvest handling to lift overall value.

8 公頃可分區配置不同作物比例，降低風險、拉高整體現金流穩定度。 The 8-ha site can be zoned with different crop ratios to diversify risk and stabilize cash flow.

開發時程與階段規劃 Development timeline & phases

從土地整備、品種導入到穩定產期，分階段降低風險、逐步放大收益。 From land preparation and planting to stable production, the project is rolled out in phases to manage risk and grow returns step by step.

  1. 1

    第 1 階段（第 0–1 年） Phase 1 (Year 0–1)

    土地整備與基礎建設 Land preparation & basic infrastructure

    土地整理、排水與道路規劃，水源系統與基本集貨設施建置，完成品種與栽培模式設計。 Land levelling, drainage and road layout, water system and basic collection facilities, and finalizing variety and cropping designs.

  2. 2

    第 2 階段（第 1–3 年） Phase 2 (Year 1–3)

    種植導入與技術示範 Planting & technical demonstration

    完成芒果、鳳梨、釋迦等主力作物種植，建立台菲技術交流機制與在地農民培訓課程。 Establish the main mango, pineapple and sugar apple blocks, and build a Taiwan–Philippines training and demo platform for local farmers.

  3. 3

    第 3 階段（第 3–5 年） Phase 3 (Year 3–5)

    穩定產期與市場拓展 Stable production & market expansion

    逐步進入穩定產量期，強化品牌與通路合作，評估加工與出口機會，提升整體收益結構。 Move into stable bearing years, strengthen branding and channels, and explore processing and export opportunities to upgrade the revenue mix.
